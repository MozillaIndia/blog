On November 10, Firefox will celebrate 10 years of being your trusted partner in the online world. Firefox is the only non-profit, non-corporate, non-compromised browser out there, and we want Web users to everywhere know they have an independent choice.

We're looking for help in spreading the word so we've created a guide on how to participate firefox10.org.

Has limited time?  Here are the two priorities.

  • Zero time - Sign up for the Firefox Thunderclap: Share the message that you - along with thousands of people around the world - choose independent.
    Sign Up Now!

  • A little more time - Take a picture and share it: Put on Firefox gear, select a landmark in your city, print out this sign (link to sign), rally your friends and family, take a photo, and share it across your social networks. Make sure to caption it with: Firefox is my independent choice in (select city). #ChooseIndependent, #fx10
    We have localized the above graphics to Hindi. You can find the front side and the back side of the graphic and use it in Hindi.

Both actions will show the breadth and reach of Mozillians!

Stay tuned for more details on November 10th - including how to participate in launch activities locally and from afar.

Let's light this fire!
