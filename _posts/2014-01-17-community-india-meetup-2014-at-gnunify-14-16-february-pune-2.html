Hello all, here is some exciting news!

Community India Meetup 2014 is scheduled for 16 February at Pune during GNUnify (14-16 February). We invite all the active and core contributors of Mozilla in India to join the meetup to participate in discussions, share experiences, practices and help build the road-map for 2014.

[caption id="attachment_763" align="alignleft" width="600"] Mozilla India Community @ GNUnify'13[/caption]

About Community India Meetup :  Mozilla India regional community participates at GNUnify every year to help, learn & teach the Open Web ideologies, Mozilla mission.
Along with participating at the conference we, the regional community have been hosting our pan-India meetup during the same time.
Community India Meetup at GNUnify'14 would be a gathering of community members from India who will share their ideas to build a road-map of activities which would play a key role in making 2014 a highly successful year for Mozilla India Community.

Read about Community India Meetup 2013 during Gnunify'13

About GNUnify'14 : GNUnify is an annual gathering consisting of Free and Open Source software workshops, talks, seminars and BOFs (Birds of a feather), hosted by the Pune Linux User Group(PLUG) and the Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research(SICSR) in Pune, India.
It is an amazing opportunity for people from foss communities all over the India to meet each other and a good chance for newbees to find out how to get involved in foss projects and learn more about the free and open source culture. Mozilla has been actively participating at GNUnify since 2008!
More about Gnunify: http://www.gnunify.in
Twitter: @gnunify
