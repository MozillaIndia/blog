This is a team that works with pixels mostly in front of screens sitting in places around connected via internet. We just thought to sit in a room to find out what will happen.

Walking into the unknown

Getting work done was never an issue, so identifying the challenges was the point of meeting. It took half a day to get through those.

Issues identified involves both general and specific ones, ranging from loss in translation, sticking to brand guidelines and more.

Navigating the Unknown

Navigating the unknown paths without a guide or reference is adventurous, but time consuming, which we don’t have, so we invited the Local community and shared the issues to get feedback it was a success so far. Thank you Mozilla Bengaluru.
